# Two-Digit Addition Without Regrouping - Enrichment Level

## Multi-Digit Problems
1. 24 + 13 = ?
2. 47 + 32 = ?
3. 61 + 28 = ?
4. 75 + 14 = ?
5. 83 + 16 = ?

## Challenge Word Problems
1. Sarah has 24 apples and her friend gives her 13 more. How many apples does she have now?
2. A bookstore had 47 fiction books and 32 non-fiction books. How many books are there in total?
3. In a class of 30 students, 15 are boys and 16 are girls. If 4 more boys join, how many boys are there now?

## Application Tasks
1. You are planning a party and need to buy 75 balloons. If you have 14 balloons at home, how many more do you need to buy?
2. A gardener has 83 flowers and wants to plant 16 more. How many flowers will there be in total after planting?
3. During a sale, a shop sells 61 toys on the first day and 28 toys on the second day. How many toys were sold during the two days?
